Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, gender, and profile photo.
  • Emergency Contacts/Recovery contact: Contact names and phone numbers you choose to add or import.
  • Support/Recovery/Health Information: Any information you provide in profile, Check-in, Emotional reflection, recovery, or support-related features.

Sensitive Information

  • Audio Recordings: Recordings created by you through App features.

Third-Party Services and Integrations

RecoveryMode may use trusted third-party service providers to support features such as:

If subscription services are enabled, payment processing is handled securely by authorized billing providers (such as Apple App Store or Google Play), and we do not store full payment card details on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate core App functionality
  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable emergency/safety features you trigger
  • Improve product performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal information only as long as needed for legitimate business or legal purposes.

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active.
  • Audio Recordings and User Content: Retained until you delete them, delete your account, or as required by law.
  • Emergency Contact Data: Retained while your account is active or until removed by you.
  • Location Data: Used temporarily for emergency/safety functionality and generally not stored long-term unless needed for security, legal, or incident-handling purposes.

Account Deletion

You may request account deletion through the App settings or by contacting us. After verification, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable timeframe (typically within 24-48 hours, except where retention is required by law).

Data Linked to You

RecoveryMode may collect and link the following data to your identity for app functionality:

  • Health & Fitness (Health): Recovery and wellness-related information you provide or generate in the app.
  • Contact Info (Name, Email Address): Used to create, manage, and support your account.
  • User Content (Photos or Videos, Audio Data): Content you upload, record, or share through app features.
  • Contacts: Contact details you add or import for support, communication, or emergency-related features.
  • Location (Precise Location): Collected only when required for app features you activate (such as safety or location-based support tools).

All of the above data is used for App Functionality and is linked to your identity.
