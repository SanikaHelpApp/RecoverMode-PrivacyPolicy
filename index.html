Recovery Mode Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Recovery Mode (the “App”). Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use Recovery Mode. By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information Provided by You

  • Account Information: Name, email address, login credentials, and profile details (such as profile photo where you choose to add one).
  • Recovery and Wellness Information: Recovery-related check-ins, reflections, goals, preferences, and similar content you enter in the App.
  • Care and Support Context: Information you share to coordinate support, including communications with your care team where those features are enabled.

Contacts and Communication

  • Contacts (optional): Names and contact details you select when using recovery or emergency-style contact features.
  • In-App Messages: Messages and related metadata sent or received through App messaging, where available.
  • Notifications: Delivery and interaction data for push notifications and reminders you opt into.

Sensitive and Device-Related Information

  • Location Data: Approximate and/or precise location, including when Recovery Mode or related safety features are active. Depending on your settings, location may be processed in the foreground and/or background to support zone or safety workflows (for example, safe-zone awareness). Location-derived signals (such as zone classification) may be used only to power App functionality you enable.
  • Camera and Microphone (optional): Used when you start or join user-initiated live video or audio sessions with your care team (for example, via our real-time video provider). The App does not continuously access the camera or microphone in the background outside features you explicitly use.
  • Photos / Media (where used): Images or files you choose to attach or share through supported features.

Technical and Service Data

  • Device and Diagnostics: Device type, operating system, app version, identifiers needed for authentication or push notifications, and limited log, crash, or performance data to keep the App reliable and secure.

Third-Party Services and Integrations

Depending on the features you use, data may be processed by providers such as:

  • Authentication, database, and backend hosting (for example, cloud infrastructure used to store account and App content you sync).
  • Push notification services (for example, platform push providers).
  • Realtime video / audio (for example, when you join a live session).
  • AI or speech-related features (for example, where the App offers optional guidance or transcription-style features powered by third-party models).

Note: Recovery Mode may offer subscription or paid features in the future. When subscriptions are available, payment information will be processed securely through Google Play’s billing system, Apple’s in-app purchase system, or other authorized payment processors. Users will have the option to manage or cancel subscriptions according to platform rules.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide App functionality, including accounts, recovery tools, Recovery Mode and related safety workflows, messaging, and notifications.
  • Facilitate optional live audio/video sessions you initiate with your care team.
  • Deliver reminders, alerts, and service messages you request or that are necessary to operate the App.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and security, and to detect, prevent, or address misuse or technical issues.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

Data Retention and Deletion

Account and synced App content: Retained while your account is active and as needed to provide the service, resolve disputes, enforce terms, and meet legal or regulatory requirements.

Location: Processed as needed for features you enable (for example, Recovery Mode). We do not use location to build unrelated advertising profiles. Retention of location-related records, if any, is limited to what is needed for the feature or for security and legal compliance.

Optional recordings or session artifacts: If a feature stores session recordings or similar media, retention depends on the feature and your account settings; you may be able to delete certain content in the App where supported.

Contacts: Contact selections may be stored on your device, on our systems, or both, depending on the feature. You can remove or change contacts within the App where the feature allows it.

Account Deletion

You can request to delete your account through the App settings or by contacting us as described below. When you delete your account, we will delete or de-identify personal information associated with your account within a reasonable period (typically on the order of days), except where we must retain certain information to comply with law, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.
